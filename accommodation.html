ACCOMMODATION

CAMPING

· Your festival ticket includes 4 nights general camping

 

· Early camping will be allowed on Wednesday HOWEVER ONLY 400 EARLY CAMPING TICKETS WILL BE MADE AVAILABLE.  Due to the fact that we are still setting the festival up on Wednesday, we have had to limit the number of early campers, and no more than 400 will be allowed.  PLEASE NOTE THAT NO CAMPERS WILL BE ALLOWED ON SITE WITHOUT EARLY CAMPER TICKETS OR BEFORE WEDNESDAY 17th APRIL.

 

· Please also note that the taping off of areas and reserving of areas before arrival will not be allowed.

Camping is operated strictly as a first come first serve process.

 

· Those intending to use the family camping will be charged R300 per vehicle to cover extra cost of

fencing and security over the period of the festival.

 

· All campers must be off site by 14h00 on 22nd April.

A limited amount of glamping packages will be available for the weekend. They include pre-made fabric tents

suited with beds, kitchens, and private lavatories.

 

These sites will be made available upon arrival at the camp-site, and will work on a first come first serve basis unless pre-arranged

at our own discretion.

 

For pre-arrangements, contact us through our direct contact line to supply your valid reason for needing this service.

 

Only a portion of the glamping service is included in your festival ticket, the rest needs to be paid upon arrival at the site.
